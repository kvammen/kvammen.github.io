Brief

The product is completely new on the market and Geely wants to give the product a visually locking technical tone as present and future.

The landing page should provide knowledge and inspiration and has two purposes: exchange to collect email addresses for further contact and offer a page that aims to provide more images, "eye candy". How do we get the target group to leave their email?

Sketches

After the research phase I sat down with my classmate Juna Jakobsson and started to make som rough sketches for both desktop and mobile.

Wireframe

With our greybox wireframe ready, we were able to start delivering to the web developers. It was important to let the product speak for itself and we therefore chose to have large pictures and also a video. To make the page more interactive, we added a function where the user can change colors on the interior and exterior.
